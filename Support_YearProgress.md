**Common Issues**

*Widgets Not Updating*
- Widgets refresh automatically at midnight and noon
- Force refresh by opening the main app
- Check that you have granted the app necessary permissions
- Remove and re-add the widget if issues persist

*Resolution Progress Not Syncing*
- Ensure you're signed in to iCloud on all devices
- Check Settings â†’ [Your Name] â†’ iCloud â†’ iCloud Drive is enabled
- Open the app on each device to trigger sync
- Allow a few minutes for CloudKit to propagate changes

*Birthday/Age Showing Incorrectly*
- Go to Settings tab â†’ Birthday Picker
- Verify your birthday is set correctly
- Changes apply immediately to Lifetime Grid

*Can't Check In for Today*
- Make sure you're tapping on the current day in the weekly calendar
- Check that your resolution's date range includes today
- Try force-quitting and reopening the app

*Onboarding Shows Every Time*
- This may occur after app updates or reinstallation
- Complete all 4 onboarding pages to save your preference
- Use the "Skip" option if you're familiar with the app

**Privacy Policy**

This privacy policy applies to the OpenRouter Usage Monitor app (hereby referred to as "Application") for macOS created by Vijaya Kandel (hereby referred to as "Service Provider").

**Information Collection and Use**

The Application collects and displays your OpenRouter usage data, including:
- Credit balance and usage
- API requests and token counts
- Model usage breakdown

This information is fetched directly from the OpenRouter API using your API key and displayed locally on your device. The Service Provider does not collect, store, or transmit any of this data to external servers.

**Data Storage**

Your OpenRouter API key is stored securely in your Mac's Keychain. No personal data is stored by the Application.

**Third Party Access**

The Application connects directly to OpenRouter's API to fetch your usage data. Your data is subject to OpenRouter's privacy policy, available at [openrouter.ai/privacy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy).

The Service Provider does not share any information with third parties.

**Children**

The Application does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

**Changes**

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of any changes.

This privacy policy is effective as of 2026-02-14
